    Violet Fivel utilizes vibrant color combinations and bold lines for all of her artwork. Based in Baltimore, she completed her Tattoo apprenticeship in the beginning of 2003 and began watercolor painting in 2007. Her artwork reflects heavily on traditional aspects of tattooing, and her unique perspective of beauty is integrated into her compositions. An ongoing focus of her craftsmanship has been designing Folk Art. She hopes her imagery enables you to see a more beautiful world.

    Visit Violet at Read Street Tattoo Parlor.
    She's available Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays and Sundays.
